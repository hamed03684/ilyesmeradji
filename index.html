About Me

My full name is Meradji Ilyes Farouk Eddine. I live in Algeria. I've been freelancing since 2021, doing various work in the CGI industry and graphic design in general. What I make sure of is that I do my best in my work. This site is still a WIP.

I am a proficient 3D Generalist and graphic designer with over 5 years of experience using industry-standard tools such as Blender, Photoshop, and DaVinci Resolve. My skill set includes advanced proficiency in Adobe After Effects, GIMP, Marvelous Designer, Houdini, EmberGen, and Substance Painter. I specialize in texturing, UV mapping, 3D modeling, and both 2D and 3D motion graphics. Additionally, I am well-versed in Microsoft Office tools (Excel, Word, PowerPoint) and have strong programming skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and some C#. My diverse expertise allows me to tackle a wide range of creative and technical challenges efficiently.

As a 3D Generalist and freelancer for the past 4 years, I have honed my skills in delivering high-quality CGI and graphic design projects. My experience includes integrating CG elements into live footage, showcasing my expertise in visual effects (VFX). I have worked on various freelance projects since 2021, consistently meeting client expectations by combining creativity with technical precision.

I pursued my education in Accounting at Amar Telidji University in Laghouat, Algeria, where I developed a strong foundation in analytical thinking and problem-solving. While my formal education is in accounting, my passion for CGI and graphic design has driven me to self-educate and excel in these creative fields.
